My Services

I provide psychoanalytically-informed mental health support for individuals and groups, helping clients explore the deeper layers of their inner lives, understand unconscious patterns, and cultivate a sense of meaning and purpose. I also integrate other evidence-based therapies such as Cognitive Behavior Therapy and Interpersonal Therapy to assist clients in meeting their goals.

Above all, I believe in the healing power of the therapeutic relationship and am here to listen deeply, helping you rediscover your voice, strength, and direction.

Individual Therapy

In individual therapy, I work collaboratively with clients to explore the unconscious processes shaping their thoughts, emotions, and behaviors. Together, we examine internal conflicts, relational patterns, and life narratives in a safe, containing environment.

I support clients experiencing anxiety, depression, grief, relational difficulties, and life transitions, guiding them toward self-awareness, emotional integration, and resilience. Through this reflective work, clients gain insight into their inner world, uncover sources of suffering, and find renewed meaning and purpose.

Group Therapy for Pregnant and Postpartum Women

I lead groups for women navigating pregnancy and the postpartum period, focusing on the unconscious and relational dimensions of motherhood. These groups provide a reflective and supportive space to explore identity shifts, emotional ambivalence, and the complex psychological changes that accompany this transformative time.

Participants examine unconscious anxieties, relational dynamics, and evolving maternal roles while finding connection, validation, and practical strategies to foster emotional well-being.

Immigration Psychological Evaluations

I provide in-depth psychological evaluations for immigration cases, including hardship waivers, VAWA, T visas (for survivors of human trafficking), and U visas (for victims of qualifying crimes). These evaluations are conducted with clinical rigor and sensitivity, taking into account the psychological impact of trauma, loss, and displacement.

Reports are tailored to meet legal standards while reflecting a nuanced understanding of each client’s emotional and relational life, s5upporting attorneys and clients in presenting a compelling, evidence-based case.
